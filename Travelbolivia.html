Travel to....Bolivia

Bolivia is a stunning retreat. Rough around the edges, but vexing in its natural beauty, rugged and complex. Bolivia is one of South America’s most diverse and perplexing nations.

Culture

People

Bolivians are humble and loving people who love to celebrate life. Each month passes some procession or parade is celebrated honoring an important historical date or deity.

Food

Want to try llama tenderloin? Well you can in Bolivia along with a glass of up-and-coming Tarija wine, or artisanal coca or quinoa-based beer. The daily bread varies from the Frisbee-like mama qonqachi cheese bread of Cochabamba, big as your head, to the sourdough-like maraqueta hard roll, staple of paceña breakfast, to Santa Cruz’s mouthwatering cunapes (cheese bread balls).

Tips for travel
